SummaryThe terms HUL, the Company, your Company, we, our and us refer to Hindustan Unilever Limited. Our Integrated Annual Report encompasses the Strategic Reports, pages 2 to 80 and the Financial Statements, pages 81 to 201. The Strategic Report contains information about us, how we create value for our stakeholders and how we run our business. It also includes our strategy, business model, market outlook and key performance indicators.
